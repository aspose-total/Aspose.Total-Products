Firstly, by using [Aspose.PDF for .NET](https://products.aspose.com/pdf/net/), you can export PS to XLSX. After converting PS to EXCEL, [Aspose.Cells for .NET](https://products.aspose.com/cells/net/) enables you to encrypt and password protect your EXCEL documents.
