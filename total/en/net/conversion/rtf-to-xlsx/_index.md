In order to add a custom property, you can use [Add](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.properties/customdocumentpropertycollection/methods/add/index) method for the [CustomDocumentPropertyCollection](https://reference.aspose.com/cells/net/aspose.cells.properties/customdocumentpropertycollection) class.
